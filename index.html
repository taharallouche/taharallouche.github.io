About Me

I'm currently a Scientific Software Engineer at Descartes Underwriting , working on industrializing software solutions for risk assessment of wildfires and cyber attacks.

I hold a PhD from LAMSADE in Paris Scienes et Lettres university. I was advised by Jérôme Lang and Florian Yger. My thesis contributions consist of both theoritical and practical advances in crowdsourcing data labeling.

I have a mathematical engineering diploma from ENSTA-Paris and a Master degree in Optimization from the Paris Polytechnic Institute.

I like playing the guitar and I enjoy reading (and discussing) anthropology and political economy.
