About Me

I'm currently a second year PhD student at LAMSADE in Paris-Dauphine university. I'm advised by Jérôme Lang and Florian Yger.

I'm interested in computational social choice and artificial intelligence, and I've been working on epistemic voting and its application to Crowdsourcing.

I have a mathematical engineering diploma from ENSTA-Paris and a Master degree in Optimization from the Paris Polytechnic Institute.

I like playing the guitar and I enjoy reading (and discussing) anthropology and political economy.
